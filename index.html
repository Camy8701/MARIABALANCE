About Maria Balance

A calm practice for strength, breath, and control.

Maria Balance is a gentle outdoor Pilates practice in Barcelona for people who want to move with intention, build core strength, and reconnect with their body without the rush of a crowded studio.

Classes are simple, focused, and welcoming. Each session blends controlled mat work, breath-led transitions, and clear guidance so beginners feel supported and returning students can deepen their practice.
